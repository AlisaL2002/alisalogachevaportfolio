Save-a-Coin

I am a Web Design and Development student at the University of Central Florida pursuing a career in UX/UI design. My passion for product design goes beyond pleasant-looking interfaces. In my work, I focus on creating an accessible and convenient user experience. My main strategies are customer focus, clear goal visualization, design thinking, and risk identification.

My technical skills include Figma, Canva, and Sketch softwares, as well as Adobe applications. I am always eager to learn more new tools to efficiently create dynamic designs for mobile applications, websites, digital platforms, and more.

I have over two years of experience working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, React Native, and various database management systems (such as MySQL and MongoDB), as well as command-line tools. My background in web development allows for a deeper understanding of the technical aspects when designing digital interfaces. I am able to design with realistic implementation possibilities in mind, which helps build a stronger connection between the design and development teams.
