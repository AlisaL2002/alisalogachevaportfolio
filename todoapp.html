OVERVIEW

About

ToDo App is a mini project designated to demonstrate both creativity and problem-solving skills through the design of a simple digital product like a to-do app.

Project Description

  • For this project, I explored various design approaches and chose to focus on iOS UI design. I followed the Human Interface Guidelines and utilized Apple's visual elements to create a polished and cohesive UX.
  • The project challenged me to apply my quick decision-making skills to create a user interface from scratch to address specific user needs.
  • In my work, I prioritized a simple UI and intuitive navigation while incorporating a 'brand' identity with soft color palettes and unique visual elements.

Timeline

ToDo App was a small redesign project that took one day to complete in March 2024.

PROCESS

The objective of this project was to redesign a native Apple or Android app and give it a fresh, user-centered perspective. I chose to remake the iOS Reminders and Calendar apps by blending their core functionalities into a single, cohesive experience. I began with research into color psychology and user engagement strategies to identify visual elements like that could make the app feel more inviting and encouraging to use.

To improve usability and planning efficiency, I made the interface calendar-oriented, allowing users to naturally connect tasks with specific dates. This structure not only streamlined task management but also gave the app a more organized intuitive feel.

OUTCOMES

Results

The result was a clean Figma prototype that reimagines the native iOS Reminders and Calendar apps with a fresh, user-friendly design. The final design successfully showcases thoughtful UX decisions, a soft and inviting visual style, and a calendar-focused layout that makes task organization feel effortless.
