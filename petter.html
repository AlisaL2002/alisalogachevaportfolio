About

Petter is an app designed to help pet owners manage animal care.

Key features:

  • Creating pet profiles, including medical history and other characteristics that will make pet stores, groomers, and vet clinic visits easier.
  • Pets' feeding schedule and noting food amounts to promote a healthier diet.
  • Tracking pet exercise routine and finding new dog parks in the area.
  • Finding the best pet products on the market.
  • Scheduling vet appointments and setting reminders.

The app's robust progression trackers allow users to monitor pet care activities and needs, making taking care of your pets easy and stress-free.
