PROJECT
OVERVIEW

About

PETTER is a senior group project completed at the University of Central Florida as part of the Digital Media program. It is designed to help pet owners manage animal care by streamlining pet-related tasks and ensuring a seamless experience.

Key Features

  • Creating pet profiles, including medical history and other characteristics that will make pet stores, groomers, and vet clinic visits easier.
  • Pets' feeding schedule and noting food amounts to promote a healthier diet.
  • Tracking pet exercise routines and finding new dog parks in the area.
  • Finding the best pet products on the market.
  • Scheduling vet appointments and setting reminders.

Timeline

The project spanned from August 2024 to April 2025, with the final product being presented in April 2024 to all University program graduates and faculty.

PROCESS

Discovery Phase

We conducted a thorough analysis of existing pet management apps to identify gaps in the market and opportunities for innovation. Our research revealed that no single digital product currently offers a comprehensive suite of resources for both new and existing pet owners.

This finding underscored the need for an app like PETTER, designed to organize and streamline the pet care experience, making it more manageable and stress-free for owners.

Research

Targeting current pet owners and prospective pet parents, we gathered qualitative data through surveys and structured interviews to understand their daily struggles, needs, and desires in managing pet care.

Based on these insights, we developed detailed personas representing our appâ€™s primary users, which guided our design decisions.

Design Process

Our objective in the design phase was to create a user-friendly and visually appealing interface that meet accessibility guidelines and would make using the application seamless and intuitive. Figma was our main design and collaboration tool at this stage of the project.

Throughout the design process, we engaged with potential users to gather feedback on our prototypes. This feedback was crucial in refining the design before finalizing the screens.

Development Process

The goal of the development phase was to turn our designs into a fully functional app that is robust, responsive, and scalable. Working in React Native ensured a smooth and native-like experience across both iOS and Android platforms.

Firebase was selected as our backend database solution for its real-time data synchronization and ease of integration with React Native.
