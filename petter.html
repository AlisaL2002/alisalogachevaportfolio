PETTER

Pet care application for owners

Project
Overview

background

Petter is an app designed to help pet owners manage animal care more efficiently. It streamlines pet-related tasks, ensuring a seamless experience for pet owners.

goal

The project aims to enhance pet care management by integrating automated scheduling, feeding tracking, and health monitoring features.

timeline

The project started in August 2024 and is currently in development, with planned feature rollouts in the coming months.
