About

The Flashcard app is designed to help students effectively study for exams and master concepts with ease.

This is one of my first design projects completed in Figma. I included it to showcase the progress in my work from when I was just learning to use various design tools compared to now.

Project Description

  • The app allows users to keep their flashcards well-organized and accessible on the go.
  • Users can 'star' important cards to highlight concepts they want to revisit or focus on.
  • While studying, users can mark flashcards with a checkmark or an X, enabling them to sort cards based on their understanding and tailor future study sessions accordingly.
  • The app is meant to display progress metrics, like the number of cards memorized, providing motivation and insight into the userâ€™s learning journey.

Timeline

Flashcard App was designed in February 2023.

PROCESS

The primary goal of this project was to experiment with various layout styles and UI components to create a visually appealing and functional app for organizing, storing, and studying flashcards in one convenient place. I explored different approaches, including list-based layouts, grid views with card lists, and single-card displays centered on the screen. Additionally, I incorporated a range of button styles to enhance interactivity and provide clear navigation.

OUTCOMES

Results

This hands-on experimentation allowed me to explore and practice various UI design techniques, resulting in a complete Figma design.
