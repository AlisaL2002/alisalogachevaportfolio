About

Workout app is an application aimed at helping users organize their workout routine, maintaining consistency in their sessions, and making the entire process stress-free.

Project Description

  • Created as a mini project to practice designing an application using a single-shade color palette while still meeting accessibility requirements, resulting in a user-centered experience.
  • Opting for a simple design featuring sticker-style visual elements, I utilized negative space to create a minimalistic interface that would not overwhelm the user.
  • Focusing on delivering a clear navigation system, ensuring users can seamlessly plan, track, and manage their workouts.

Timeline

ToDo App was a small design project that took place in March 2024.

PROCESS

The Workout App design process began by defining the core features based on the given requirements: the app needed to include at least one timer, an iteration counter, three different workout types, and a distinct visual style with a custom color palette.

I chose to focus the app around the exercises themselves, incorporating images of people performing each workout to create a clean design and a motivating experience for users. To maintain a cohesive aesthetic, I used a single-shade color palette that balanced minimalism with clarity while ensuring accessibility.

OUTCOMES

Results

The final design emphasized simplicity and user engagement, making it easy to track and complete workouts. The resulting product is a Figma prototype.
