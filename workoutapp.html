Workout App

Project information

  • Category: App design
  • Project date: Mar 2024
  • Project URL: Figma Design

About

Workout app is an application aimed at helping users organize their workout routine, maintaining consistency in their sessions, and making the entire process stress-free.

Project description:

  • Created as a mini project to practice designing an application using a single-shade color palette while still meeting accessibility requirements, resulting in a user-centered experience.
  • Opting for a simple design featuring sticker-style visual elements, I utilized negative space to create a minimalistic interface that would not overwhelm the user.
  • Focusing on delivering a clear navigation system, ensuring users can seamlessly plan, track, and manage their workouts.
